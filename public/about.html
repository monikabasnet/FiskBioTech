Who we are

Mission & Vision

Mission: empower students to explore biotechnology through projects, peer-led workshops, and industry mentorship. Vision: an inclusive community pushing the boundaries of biotech and making science accessible.

History

Founded in 2019, the club has hosted 40+ events, published student posters, and partnered with labs and startups. Our alumni now work in wet labs, bioinformatics, and biotech product teams.

Achievements
  • Annual symposium with invited biotech speakers
  • Student CRISPR demo & wet-lab bootcamps
  • Open-source bioinformatics pipelines published on GitHub
